1. DECIDING TO WEAR A JACKET OR NOT
    unlock phone
    open weather app
    check current temperature
    IF current temperature is greater than 65 THEN
        no jacket needed
    ELSE
        put on jacket
    END
    
2. As a cyclist, how many bikes should you own?
    n = number of bikes you currently own
    savings = amount of dollars in your savings account
    price = price of new bike
    
    WHILE savings > price THEN
        n = n + 1
        savings = savings - price
    END
